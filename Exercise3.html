II."Washing"

That is right you need to wash youre rice before cooking to reduce the excess starch here are some tips:

  1. Wash the Rice until its not couldy
  2. Do not put less water than the rice when washing because that would be diffcult to wash
  3. wash it roughly not gently
    4. III."Water Measurements"

    It is very important to know how much water you need to put so that the rice will cook perfectly, you dont want the rice water to more than or less than its measurement and there are some technic on ways how we measure it and here are some tips:

    1. Its very traditional for asians to use the "Finger Tactic", basically measure on the top of the rice until it reach the first line of the index/middle finger
    2. The other meathod is using the same or replica of the cup you use to measure the rice for watering doubling on how much you put in
      3. IV."Cooking Prosidures"

      Finaly the cooking prosidures, it is very i,portant to know how we cook the rice so that it wont get burnt or uncook here are prosidures to follow:

      1. At first turn the heat meduim heat
      2. then wait for it to boil
      3. After it boil make sure its not watery, wait for the right consistensy
      4. When it reach to the right consistensy turn the heat to low for about 3 - 5 mins
      5. Finally its ready to serve
